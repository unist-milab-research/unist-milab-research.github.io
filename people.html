Faculty

Arda Senocak

Assistant Professor

Artificial Intelligence Graduate School, UNIST

Prof. Arda Senocak leads the Multisensory Intelligence Lab at UNIST Graduate School of AI. His research focuses on multimodal learning, developing machine perception models that learn from visual, audio, and other sensory inputs to interact effectively with the world. He holds a Ph.D. and M.S. from KAIST under the supervision of Prof. In So Kweon, and a B.S. in Computer Science from KAIST.
